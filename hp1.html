LabTrack

A minimal, web-based inventory management system designed for the senior high school laboratory. Elegant, fast, and easy to use.

The Researchers

We are a group of STEM InfoTech students from Pasig Catholic College, and this project, LabTrack, was developed as part of our Practical Research 2 course.

LabTrack is a web-based inventory management system prototype designed to assist Senior High School science teachers in monitoring and managing laboratory equipment.

Schedule

LabTrack also features a scheduling function that allows teachers to reserve laboratory equipment and monitor availability.

Reports

The reporting feature of LabTrack enables teachers and administrators to generate summaries of laboratory usage and inventory status.
